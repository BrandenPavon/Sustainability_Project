ℹ️ About

SusCalc v1.0 is a carbon footprint calculator designed to help you understand the annual CO₂ impact of your everyday habits.

Simply fill in the Calculator window with your daily routines and hit Calculate Impact to see a full breakdown.

📐 How we calculate:

🚿 Shower 0.05 kg per min/yr
🥩 Red Meat 3.0 kg per meal
🚗 Car 0.21 kg per mile
✈️ Flight 255 kg per flight
👕 Fashion 7.0 kg per item

Emission factors sourced from publicly available lifecycle assessment data. Results are estimates for educational purposes.

🌍 Our Mission

Our mission to help people become more conscious about how their decisions can contribute to the environment, whether positively or negatively. We want to bring these invisible impacts into focus and make people aware which is always the first step. "Sustainability" does not mean sacrificing everything and living in the woods but smart and intentional ways to make our environment cleaner and safer for everyone.

💡 Eco Tips

🚿 Showers: Cutting 2 min saves ~36 kg CO₂/yr.
🥩 Meat: One meatless day/week saves ~156 kg CO₂/yr.
🚗 Car: Carpooling halves your driving emissions.
✈️ Flights: One fewer flight saves ~255 kg CO₂.
👕 Fashion: Buy second-hand and save ~7 kg per item.
🌱 The average person emits ~4–8 tons of CO₂ per year.
🎯 Aim for under 2 tons to align with climate goals.
