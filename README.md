# SusCalc 🌱
**Self Sustainability. Calculated and Curated. Made by Branden Pavon and Sean Sotto**

SusCalc is a web-based carbon footprint calculator and resource hub that helps everyday consumers understand how their daily habits contribute to their environmental impact — and what they can do about it.

🌐 **Live Site:** [suscalc.org](https://suscalc.org)

---

## The Problem

Many people want to live more sustainably but face common barriers:

- Lack of clear, personalized guidance on where to start
- Difficulty identifying which daily habits contribute most to their carbon footprint
- No simple tools that turn awareness into concrete, actionable steps

---

## The Solution

SusCalc makes sustainability accessible by combining a fast, intuitive calculator with curated resources and actionable next steps. Key features include:

- **Personalized Carbon Footprint Breakdown** — Adjust sliders or enter precise values to calculate your environmental impact based on real daily habits
- **Actionable Recommendations** — Get direct links to secondhand clothing, public transit resources, and curated educational content
- **Streamlined Information Gathering** — Simple, concise interface designed for everyday users, not experts
- **Interactive Mini-Game** — Sort falling objects into the trash or recycle bin while you wait for results
- **Mobile Support** — Fully responsive design with a mobile-friendly home bar layout

---

## How It Works

1. **Visit** [suscalc.org](https://suscalc.org)
2. **Adjust** the sliders to reflect your daily habits, or click **"Precise"** to enter exact values
3. **Click "Calculate Impact"** to see your personalized carbon footprint results
4. **Explore recommendations** and linked sustainability resources
